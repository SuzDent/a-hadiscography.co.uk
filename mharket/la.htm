Los Angeles (11.03.96)

This German made 5" CD-single was commercially released in Norway only. The cover is beige with a picture of Morten and "LA" printed in large light beige letters up the right hand side. In addition, "Morten Harket" is written in white letters on a black background across the middle on the right side and "Los Angeles" is written in black along the "L". The spine is beige with black titles.
The reverse of the sleeve features aqua blue water with "Los A" printed in light blue large letters along the bottom so that the `water' can be seen through. The track listing is printed in white on black uneven shaped strips and there is additional white text underneath.
The disc is of a peachy colour with "LA" in large silver letters and titles in black. The CD features album tracks only.
