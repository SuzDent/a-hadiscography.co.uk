A Jester In Our Town (1999)

This 1 track promo only CD "A Jester in our Town" was released by Polydor. The song is taken from the musical Sophie's World and is performed by Morten. It can also be found on the soundtrack album Sophie's World. The CD cover is much the same as the album cover, featuring a blue and pink picture of the left-hand side of a girl's face wearing a globe ear ring. The title is printed in white across the middle of the sleeve.
