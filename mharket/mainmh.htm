Morten Harket Discography

www.a-hadiscography.co.uk/mortenh.htm

Welcome to the web discography for Morten Harket - Norwegian solo artist and lead singer with the band a-ha. Follow the links to the separate discography of a-ha and other related projects.

Morten released his fifth solo album Out Of My Hands in parts of Europe on 13 April and 14 May in the UK. The first single Lightning premiered on Norwegian radio on 13 February 2012 and was released in Norway on 9 March 2012. The single Scared Of Heights was released in Germany on 30 March and 10 April in the UK. The second single in the UK was the promo only I'm The One released on 9 July.
