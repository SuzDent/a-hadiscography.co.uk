Waiting For Her (25.05.91)

This Japanese only release is available on 3" CD which comes in a snap-pack. The top half of the front of the sleeve is similar to the cover of the album "East Of The Sun West Of The Moon" with "Gatsby" written in cream coloured letters on a blue strip along the top. The bottom half of the cover is peacock blue coloured with light purple "Waiting For Her"-title and other cream and white text.
The reverse of the snap-pack is white with black text, including the song lyrics in English and Japanese and a tiny black and white picture of the album. The CD is silver coloured with red WB-logo, black text and small black a-ha-logo.
