a-ha Tour Brasil - Agosto 2002 (08.02)


This rare Brazilian promo only 5-track sampler CD was issued in Brazil to promote the August 2002. It features four album tracks (Forever Not Yours (4:06) / Take On Me (3:46) / You are The One (3:48) / Crying In The Rain (4:25)) plus a live version of "Hunting High And Low" (7:07) recorded in Oslo in 2001. It comes in a stickered slimline jewel case with custom title sleeve and printed disc. The cover is dark red with the a-ha logo in white across the centre and title in black below.
