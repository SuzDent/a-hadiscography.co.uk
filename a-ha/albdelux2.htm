Deluxe Editions (27.09.19)

On 27 September 2019, two further deluxe editions of a-ha albums were released, "Minor Earth | Major Sky" and "Lifeline". Both releases come in card fold-out digi sleeves with similar front artwork to the original editions, They have been remastered and have two discs, the first with the album plus a bonus tracks and a second disc with rare, unreleased and alternative versions.
