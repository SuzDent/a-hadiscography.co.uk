Hunting High and Low Super Deluxe Box Set (24.02.23)

On the 24th February 2023, "Hunting High And Low" was released by BMG as a special 6 LP Super Deluxe Box Set. The LPs come in an LP sized hard cardboard slip box along with a 64-page LP sized booklet. On the front of the cellophane is a red sticker with the text "a-ha hunting high and low 6LP SUPER DELUXE BOX SET Includes rare demos, remixes and alternative mixes, plus 64-page booklet BMGCAT575BOX BMG". The tracks are the same as those on the expanded edition.

The LPs come inside 4 card sleeves, the first LP of the regular LP comes with similar artwork to the original release. It comes in the usual picture LP sleeve. The labels are white with titles in grey and red. The other LPs come in white sleeves with sketches taken from the "Take On Me" video; LPs 2 and 3 feature the three band members, 4 and 5 Morten and Bunty separated by glass and 6 motorbikes lined up at a starting line. Above the scretches, the title is printed with a large grey a-ha logo, "hunting high and low" in red and a sub-title in grey underneath. LPs 2-3 "the demos 1982-1984"; LPs 4-5 "singles, extended versions & b-sides"" and LP6 "the alternate mixes".

The reverse of sleeves 2-6 are white with printed hand drawn lines making a border around the edge and barcode. The track list is printed in grey on the left hand side with the track numbers in red, labelled k1-k5 and l1-l5. The LPs come in plain white paper sleeves which come inside the card sleeves. LP labels 2-3 are white with titles in grey and red and LP labelss 4-6 are black with the titles in white, grey and red. The record sides of the LPs are labelled a, b, c, d, e, f, g, h, i, j, k and l respectively.
