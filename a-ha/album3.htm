East Of The Sun West Of The Moon (22.10.90)

The front of the CD insert features a black and white group photo. Inside the booklet are 12 black and white pictures plus song lyrics. The back insert of the CD is black with grey "a-ha" writing, purple rectangle and silver titles. The CD is silver with purple titles and "a-ha" logo (in similar font to that on the front sleeve). The LP has a black inner sleeve with 3 individual pictures and lyrics. The album is produced by Chris Neil, except for * by Ian Stanley.

The German made cassette has cover similar to the LP and CD. The insert folds out with the lyrics printed in black on a white background. There are also three black and white individual shots of the band members plus further information in white and yellow text on a purple background. The spine of the insert is white with black titles. The actual cassette is transparent / grey with white titles. The UK cassette comes with a white circular sticker with black text "Includes CRYING IN THE RAIN and I CALL YOUR NAME 7599-26314-4 WX378C".

There were at least two different pressings of the CD available in Germany. Both have a similar sleeve design, but the actual discs differ. The first has the a-ha logo printed using the same font as that on the front sleeve. The second version has the a-ha logo printed in plain purple capital letters. Both versions have the same catalogue number.

Tracks: Crying In The Rain (4:25) / Early Morning* (2:59) / I Call Your Name (4:54) / Slender Frame (3:42) / East Of The Sun* (4:47) / Sycamore Leaves (5:22) / Waiting For Her* (4:49) / Cold River (4:40) / The Way We Talk* (1:30) / Rolling Thunder (5:43) / (Seemingly) Non-stop July* (2:55).
