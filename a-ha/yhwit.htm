You Have What It Takes (16.09.22)

a-ha release their second single "You Have What It Takes" from their album &Quot;True North" as a download release. The release includes two versions of the track: "You Have What It Takes (Radio Edit)" (3:35) and "You Have What It Takes" (04:22)

The cover of the single uses the same artwork as the album, white with a shot of, presumably the arctic, sea breaking on to the beach in the middle. Above is the a-ha logo in grey and the title below.
