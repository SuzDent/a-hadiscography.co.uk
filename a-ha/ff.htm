Forest Fire (.11.15)

   

In November 2015, the song "Forest Fire" was released as a promo only single in the UK. It comes in a pvc sleeve with insert and features the album version (3:59). The cover artwork is white with the lines from the inside of a tree trunk in faded grey. The a-ha logo is in large grey letters at the top with the title underneath. The reverse of the insert is white with titles in black. The disc is silver with black titles.
