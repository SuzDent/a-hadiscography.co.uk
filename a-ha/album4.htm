a-ha Albums 1993-99


Memorial Beach / The Selections

Memorial Beach (14.06.93)

This 10-track album was written by Pål Waaktaar, except "Move To Memphis" by Pål and Magne Furuholmen and "Lamb To The Slaughter" by Magne. The album was produced by David Z and a-ha and mixed by Rod Hui with Pål Waaktaar. It features Morten on vocals, Magne on keyboards, Pål on guitar, J.B. Bøgeberg on bass, Per Hillestad on drums and JD Steele, Jevetta Steele and Kathy Wilson on backing vocals on "Lie Down In Darkness" and "Move To Memphis". This German made album was available in many European countries.

The cover of the LP features a large red and orange a-ha-logo which appears to be glowing through the undergrowth. Underneath there is a colour-shot by Just Loomis of a-ha standing on a beach (Pål, Morten and Magne). The title is written in small white letters at the top. The reverse of the LP features more of the red and black 'undergrowth' with a black and white shot of the band in the bottom section (Magne, Morten and Paul - similar to that on "The Blood That Moves The Body '92" single). The tracks are listed in white above the picture. It comes with an inner sheet with a large black and white a-ha picture and the lyrics on the back. The LP version of the album was not available in the UK but could be imported from Germany.

The CD edition has similar cover to the LP. It comes with an 8-page booklet. The pages of this booklet have a grey and black patterned background design. Inside the cover there is a photograph of a woman lying on a bed and the track listing is written in white on the facing page. In the centre there is a large black and white band-shot (Magne, Morten and Pål). The other pages feature the lyrics in white text and other black and white group shots. Early pressings of the CD booklet have the lyrics for "Memorial Beach" missing, although they are present in later pressings. The reverse of the booklet has a picture of a red and orange locust `glowing' through the undergrowth. The back insert has a different colour-shot of a-ha standing on the beach. The tracks are written in white on a black background underneath and "Produced by David Z and a-ha" printed in red. The CD is grey with a picture of a locust in white at the bottom and other text in white. The matrix code of this release is: 936245229-2. There were at least two further pressings of the CD album. The second pressing is printed using a slightly lighter shade of charcoal than the first and the matrix code, printed around the centre of the disc is: 936245229-2.2 V01. The third pressing is printed in much darker, almost black shaded of grey and the matrix number is 936245229-2.3 V01.

The German made cassette has a similar cover to the LP and CD. The sleeve folds out with the song lyrics printed in black over the top of a very pale undergrowth shot on one side. The other side features the black and white group shot found in the centre of the CD booklet, a glowing red and yellow locust with text in white printed over the top plus the black and white picture of the woman lying on the bed with track listing in white above. The spine of the cassette sleeve is black with white text. The actual cassette is transparent / grey with white titles.

Tracks: Dark Is The Night For All (3:46) / Move To Memphis (4:22) / Cold As Stone (8:19) / Angel In The Snow (4:13) / Locust (5:09) // Lie Down In Darkness (4:32) / How Sweet It Was (6:00) / Lamb To The Slaughter (4:20) / Between Your Mama And Yourself (4:16) / Memorial Beach (4:36).

There is a WEA records promo "TapetoTape" cassette which comes with a white paper insert. The "TapetoTape" logo is printed in black at the top with the tracks listed below. It has the same track listing as the regular release.

Variations

Argentina: The Argentina cassette is similar to the European edition, but the back of the folded cassette insert has all tracks listed in Spanish:
Lado 1: la noche es oscura / mudarse a Memphis / frio como una roca / angel en la nieve / langosta
Lado 2: recuestate en la oscuridad / cuan dulce era / el cordero del sacrificio / entre vos y tu mama / memorial beach

Unlike the European edition, there is no barcode on the cover. There is a "WARNER MUSIC ARGENTINA" and some Spanish copyright and manufacture information on the back sleeve. The font size of the title on the spine is much bigger than the European release and the WB logo is at the top with the catalogue number and Warner Music Argentina logo at the bottom. The folded insert is similar to the European edition but printed slightly darker grey and with some Spanish record company information. The cassette itself is transparent with grey text which is again printed in a larger font. All of the tracks are listed in Spanish.

Brazil: The first pressing of the album is similar to the European release, but comes with a black disc. The tracks are listed in silver down the centre of the disc (above and below the centre hole). The title is printed on the left and side of the disc with the record company and compact disc logos on the right, There is also some Brazil manufacture information around the disc on the right hand side. Apart from the catalogue number the booklet is the similar to the European edition. Each page is numbered, but pages 13 and 14 with the Memorial Beach lyrics and pictures of Morten and Paul are missing! The back cover includes additional "1993 WARNER MUSIC BROS RECORDS INC" text in red plus Brazil distribution information.

The Brazilian cassette is manufactured by Warner Music Brazil Ltda. The cover is similar to the European release. The cassette is transparent / grey with white titles. A unique number is printed after each track on the cassette.

Canada: The Canadian CD, released 14 June 1993, of "Memorial Beach" is similar to the second European pressing with a more charcoal coloured lyric booklet. The disc is black with a white locust, rather than grey with grey and white. There is no manufacturing or copyright information around the outer edge of the disc, instead the manufacturing information is printed on the left hand side, with the WB, CD logos and catalogue number on the right. The bar code on the back sleeve is smaller than on the European pressing and without catalogue number underneath. Along the bottom of the back sleeve is manufacturing and copyright information.

Croatia: The cassette is similar to the European release but with "Croatia Records" logo at the top of the spine with catalogue number underneath. There is also some Croatian Record company text on the back cover. The cassette itself is black with bright yellow labels and black text.

Czech Republic: There exists a Czech Republic CD of "Memorial Beach" which was distributed by POPRON and licensed by Warner Bros. "Popron" is printed in white letters in the bottom right hand corner of the back sleeve. Otherwise the sleeve design is similar to the European release. The code 50 233-2 is printed underneath poprun on the disc.

Germany: There is a slightly different pressing of "Memorial Beach" in Germany which has an additional barcode on the reverse of the sleeve with a different catalogue number. This number has also been printed on the back of the CD booklet.

There was also a unique promo only item available in Germany. It consists of the standard album "Headlines And Deadlines", the standard "Dark Is The Night" German single plus 11 special paper inserts (all 1 sided except no. 4 - see below) and the Headlines and Deadlines album booklet. These inserts come in the pockets of a black a-ha CD carry case. The a-ha logo is printed in white letters on the front of the case with "Case Logic" in white embroided letters at the bottom.

Row 1 left: Memorial Beach Cover, Row 1 middle: Advert for Memorial Beach, Row 1 right: Memorial Beach album credits, Row 2 left: Dark Is The Night cover, Row 2 middle: Dark Is The Night Single advert, Row 2 right: MTV Campaign adverts, Row 3 left: Promotion, Press and Tour advert, Row 3 middle: MTV / TV appearances, Row 3 right: Biography, Row 4 left: Live In South America video advert, Row 4 middle: Album Discography, Row 4 right: Headlines and Deadlines 12 page album booklet

Below: left: Dark Is The Night credits (reverse of insert 4 above), middle: Dark Is The Night disc, right Headlines and Deadlines disc

Slight variations on this promo item exist with slightly different paper inserts plus three framed Diascope slides and 1 large photo negative slides. Some of the 11 paper inserts also differ slightly.

Indonesia: The Indonesian cassette is similar to the German made release but includes the text "Hanya Untuk Dijual Di Indonesia" printed in white at the bottom of the front sleeve.

Japan: The CD comes with a Japanese lyric sheet plus a white and red obi strip with red, blue, white and yellow text. As with the early pressings of the European release, the lyrics for the title track are also missing from the booklet. The album title is printed in English on one of the spines and in Japanese on the other. On the promo copy of the CD album, the text "sample not for sale" is etched in around the centre of the disc and a white and red promo-sticker is on the back insert.

 

Korea: The Korean CD of "Memorial Beach" differs slightly from the European release.

The disc is silver rather than the usual black. The disc includes the Warner Music Korea logo, plus some Sam Sung Electronics in Cooperation with HWA EUM Record Ltd information around the disc. The disc also states that it was produced by Patrick Leonard. The insert booklet is light grey, rather than dark grey and doesn't include the centre band shot. The last two pages include some Korean text, rather than the usual credits page.

On the back sleeve, the tracks are written in capital letters with track lengths included. There is no barcode or produced by David Z & A-HA text, but there is some Korea distribution and Sam Sung manufacture information.

 

The LP has similar front sleeve to the European release. The back of the sleeve is similar to the back insert of the European CD release, featuring a colour shot of the band standing on a beach with the track listing on a black background underneath. The tracks are listed in white capital letters with the track lengths printed afterwards, this differs from that used on the European CD On the front sleeve, there is a yellow sticker with some Korean lettering plus mention of Angel In The Snow and Dark Is The Night. The album comes with a picture insert.

The cassette comes in a tinted plastic case. It is manufactured by HWA EUM RECORDS LTD, MINISTRY OF CULTURE REGISTRATION NO.275. The front is purple with the regular album cover shot printed towards the top.

The insert folds out with all of the lyrics on one side on a white and grey patterned background. The other side is light pink/purple with Korean text and a pink/white shot of the band (Magne, Morten, Paul). The cassette is white with light grey labels and dark blue text. The Warner music logo is also included.

Peru: The release from Peru looks unofficial with the regular cover artwork printed in colour in the middle of the front sleeve which is otherwise plain white with black text. The title is printed in red on the spine. The tracks are listed on the fold-out part in Spanish and English. The cassette is transparent with white text plus the WB logo.

Russia: "Memorial Beach" was re-issued in a card slipcase in 2003, click here for more details. The Russian LP has a different back sleeve with the glowing locust and tracks listed in white above, but no band shot. The disc label is orange with black titles.

The CD album was re-issued in Russia by Никитин. The album is similar to the European release but comes with a slim black obi strip with text on the front and cover art for "Lifelines" and "Stay On These Roads" re-issues.

There is a Russian cassette album released by Никитин. it comes with a fold-out sleeve with just the black and white shot of the woman on the bed with track listing plus the glowing locust and text but no lyrics. The cassettes are transparent with white text.

South Africa: The disc is black with white text and doesn't have the pictures of the locust. The CD is made by CDT & Marketed & Distributed by Tusk Music Co. (Pty) Ltd. The Warner, TUSK and ARTONE logos are printed on the disc and bottom of the back sleeve.

Syria: The release from Syria looks unofficial with the regular cover artwork printed in colour at the bottom of the front sleeve on a light pink background. The spine is light green and the fold-out flaps are white. The title is printed in black at the top of the front sleeve, in bright pink on the spine and fold-out flap and in bright blue on the back flap. Th titles are printed in bright blue on the fold-out flap with a pink "Sound of Asia Est" printed stamp at the bottom.

Taiwan: This disc is made in Taiwan by UFO Group under licence from WEA International Inc. It comes with a black obi-strip with white, blue, red and pink text. The lyrics for "Between Your Mama And Yourself" are included inside the booklet. There are also two black and white text based advertising cards inside.

The Taiwan cassette comes in a UFO plastic case. The sleeve folds out in a similar way to the European release with lyrics. A one sided information card (similar to the "a-ha" one which comes with the CD) with information about the band is also included. Under the track listing on the back flap of the sleeve is some Taiwanese text plus the words "Made by UFO Group in Taiwan Under License" in white. The cassette is black with black labels and white text.

Thailand: The cassette comes with a "MGA Records Co Ltd / Warner Music International" black and white sticker. It appears to be manufactured in the US and used for the Thailand market. The cassette is transparent/grey with silver text, which is in slightly larger font than the European release and with a different layout.

Ukraine: There is a Ukrainian release of "Memorial Beach" by Odyssey. It is similar to the European release but has additional Ukranian white text at the bottom of the back cover. There is a silver hologram sticker on the back cover. The round globe "Planet of Hits" and rectangular Odyssey snake logos are printed on the back sleeve. The front sleeve has a blue and yellow rectangular box printed in the bottom right hand corner with Ukrainian text, otherwise the insert booklet is the same as the European edition. The disc is also similar to the EU edition but with some additional Ukrainian text.

USA: The U.S. CD edition is similar to the EU release. "Made in the USA" is printed on the disc but excludes the GEMA/BIEM and the copy information. The booklet and back sleeve have the US catalogue number printed on them plus the "Made in USA" text.
There is a U.S. promo cassette which comes in a clear plastic case with the Warner Music logo inscribed. It comes with a white text printed folded cardboard insert. The front cover includes title, track listing, &WB records" logo and catalogue number. The title, "WB" logo and a Californian address and telephone number is printed on the spine. The cassette itself is transparent with a white strip along the top on which the title is printed.

Venezuela: The Venezuela cassette was distributed by Sonografica. The inside of the insert(?) has the tracks listed in English and in Venezuelan with an incorrect spelling of a-ha as a-ah at the top. The cassette is black with gold text.

The Selections '85-'93 (1993)

This compilation `best of' CD was released in Japan as a radio promo only CD. The cover features the black and white band shot (Magne, Morten and Pål) which can be found on the centre pages of the "Memorial Beach" CD booklet. The `glowing' a-ha logo is placed above this picture and the title is printed on a white background at the bottom of the sleeve.

The reverse of the sleeve is black with the track listing printed in white text. The title, in white letters, is printed across the top of the sleeve.

This CD does not come with an obi strip.




Tracks: Dark Is The Night / Move To Memphis / Cold As Stone / How Sweet It Was / Take On Me / Train Of Thought / Hunting High And Low / The Sun Always Shines On TV / I've Been Losing You / Manhattan Skyline / Cry Wolf / Stay On These Roads / Touchy! / The Living Daylights / Crying In The Rain.
