Cast In Steel (12.02.16)

In February 2016, the song "Cast In Steel" was released as a download only track worldwide and features the "Steve Osborne Version" (3:36). The cover artwork is similar to the album, but is more bronze toned with the title in black and the text "STEVE OSBORNE VERSION" printed in white letters across the middle.
