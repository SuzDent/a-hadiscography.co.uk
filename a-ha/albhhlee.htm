Hunting High and Low Expanded Edition (15.11.19)

On the 15th November 2019, "Hunting High And Low" was released by Rhino Records as an expanded edition. It comes in a fold-out cardboard digi-pak. The sleeve has openings which house the 4 CDs plus booklet. The artwork is similar to the "Hunting High and Low 30th Anniversary" release and the CDs feature the same tracks.

 

The inside of the digi-pak features five drawings taken from the "Take On Me" video. The front of the 8-page double sided booklet is white with the a-ha logo in black hand drawn font with the standard red logo behind. The title is in red and black small letters below. The inside page has a picture of an original session sheet for "Take On Me" from RG Jones recording studio. The remainder of the booklet is white woth information about the album "Hunting High And Low". There is also track listings for each CD with track information and illustrations from the "Take On Me" video. The back sleeve features cover art from the "Take On Me" single releases with the third (1985) sleeve at the top. The discs are of similar design to the 30th anniversary release but with obvious title and catalogue number differences.
