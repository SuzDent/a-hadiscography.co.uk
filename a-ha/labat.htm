Living A Boy's Adventure Tale (29.09.86?) / (2005)

This promo only 7" single from The Philippines has edit versions of "Living A Boy's Adventure Tale" (3:38) on side A and "Train Of Thought" (3:40) on side B. "Living A Boy's Adventure Tale" doesn't just fade early, parts of the track have been cut, where as "Train Of Thought" just fades out earlier. It comes in a white paper sleeve. The disc has white labels with "WEA Records" printed in large letters at the top and other text, including "Promotional Copy Not For Sale" in black.

 

There is also a 12 " 2005 DJ sample remix of "Living A Boy's Adventure Tale" by Lifelike called "Adventure" (5:36). It comes in a plain grey and black "eyezcream recordings" sleeve. The disc label is bright pink with white text. Side 2 is another DJ remix "High On"
