Savoy Discography

www.a-hadiscography.co.uk/savoy.htm

Welcome to the web discography for the band Savoy consisting of Pål (Paul) Waaktaar-Savoy (vocals / guitar), Lauren Savoy (guitar / vocals) and Frode Unneland (drums). Pål Waaktaar, the Norwegian songwriter and guitarist in the band a-ha, formed Savoy in 1996. In this discography you will find detailed descriptions and pictures of the different Savoy singles, albums and promo items. It will also include other projects which Pål has been involved in. Follow the links to the separate discography of a-ha and other related projects.

Savoy are due to release a 2 disc best of album Savoy Songbook Vol. 1 in August. The double album will include some reworked versions and also some new songs. The first single will be the new song Karma Boomerang which was available for download from 13 April 2007.
