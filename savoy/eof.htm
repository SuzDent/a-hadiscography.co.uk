Empty of Feeling (15.10.04)

The second promo only single released from Savoy's album "Savoy" was "Empty of Feeling". As with "Whalebone", this promo single comes in a plain transparent sleeve and features an edit version of the song (3:18). The disc is plain silver with black titles.
