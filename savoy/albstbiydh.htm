See The Beauty In Your Drab Hometown (12.01.18)

Savoy's new ablum "See The Beauty In Your Drab Hometown" is due for release on 12 January 2018. More details to follow.

The artwork features a large black and white shot of three young people running across a car park, taken through a window. The picture fills most of the white cover with the Savoy logo top left and the title, underlined, in the top right.
