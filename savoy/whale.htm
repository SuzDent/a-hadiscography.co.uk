Whalebone (06.08.04)

The first promo only single released from Savoy's new album "Savoy" was "Whalebone". This very limited promo only CD comes in transparent plastic cover and features an edit version of the song (3:58). The disc itself is very plain with "SAVOY "WHALEBONE" [3.58]" printed in black letters at the top and track information at the bottom. The CD doesn't have a catalogue number.
