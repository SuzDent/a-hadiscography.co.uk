Singles Chart Positions

This section will eventually contain a list of release dates and chart positions of the a-ha / Morten Harket and Savoy singles plus albums from various countries. At the moment it just includes the a-ha single releases.

The tables below give the following information (from left to right): release date (given in day/month/year format), country of release, chart entry date, chart entry position, date of highest chart position, highest chart position, number of weeks in the chart, number of positions in the chart refered to. For example the third edition of "Take On Me" was released in the UK on 16th September '85. It entered the chart at number 55 on 22nd September and rose to a high of number 2 on 20th October '85. It spent a total of 19 weeks in the Top 75.
