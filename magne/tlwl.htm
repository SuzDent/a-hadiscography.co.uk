Magne Furuholmen - The Light We Lost (04.10.2019)

Magne released his second single "The Light We Lost" from his album "White Xmas Lies". This is a digital single. The artwork for the single is black with the title in large grey and light blue cut-out hand-written letters of differing sizes. It features just the one track "The Light We Lost" (04:32) taken from the album.
