Magne Furuholmen - Troubled Times (06.2020)

Magne releases the digital only single "Troubled Times" (3:54) which is a cover version of the song by "Fountains Of Wayne". The artwork for the single again features Magne's own work, a dark brown background with light coloured differently sized letters spelling out the title.

The video for the single featured short video clips sent in by fans reflecting the period during the pandemic.
