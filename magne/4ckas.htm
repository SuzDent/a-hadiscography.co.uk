Apparatjik - 4 Can Keep A Secret If 3 Of Them Are Dead (23.05.2010)

Apparatjik released the download single "4 Can Keep A Secret If 3 Of Them Are Dead". The artwork is white with an orange toned graphical illustration with the scales of the graph printed around the outside. The title is printed in black up the side of one of the scales with Apparatjik printed along the bottom one. This release features three tracks "Antlers" (03:20), "Look Kids (Premix AA) Diskjokke A" (diskjokke remix) (04:24) and "4 Can Keep A Secret If 3 Of Them Are Dead" (04:11)
