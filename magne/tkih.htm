Ti Kniver I Hjertet (Ottos Tema) (07.94)

The only single from the soundtrack album "Ti Kniver I Hjertet" was released as a promo CD for Norwegian Radio stations.
The cover of this 1-track CD by Magne Furuholmen and Kjetil Bjerkestrand with Eivind Aarset features the movie poster showing a woman holding her hands over the eyes of a man while a man is holding his hands over the mouth of a boy. The title is in red at the bottom of the sleeve while the artists names are in white at the top. The CD itself is black with red and silver titles.
