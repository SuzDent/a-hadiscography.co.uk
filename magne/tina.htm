Magne Furuholmen - This Is Now America (30.08.2019)

Magne released his first single "This Is Now America" from his forthcoming album "White Xmas Lies". This is a digital single. The artwork for the single is dark green with the title in large white cut-out hand-written font. It features just the one track "This Is Now America" (05:14) taken from the album.
