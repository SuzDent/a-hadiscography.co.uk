Magne Furuholmen - Father Christmas (24.12.2019)

Magne released his third single "Father Christmas" from his album "White Xmas Lies". This is a digital single. The artwork for the single features what looks like a dark rectangular metal plate on a white background with a handle at one end and the title in large pastel coloured (blue, green grey, orange) cut-out hand-written font. It features just the one track "Father Christmas" (03:05) taken from the album.
