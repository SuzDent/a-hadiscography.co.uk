Apparatjik - Combat Disco Music (28.03.2011)

In March 2011, Apparatjik released their download only single "Combat Disco Music" from their forthcoming album "Square Peg In A Round Hole". The artwork features the band members dressed in silver costumes including hats with horns and also gas masks with bright pink visors and holding bright green money. The release features only the title track (3:41).
