Apparatjik - Time Police (Draft 1) (21.02.2012)

Apparatjik released the promo only CD single "Time Police (Draft 1)" (3:22) on 21 February 2012. The cover features similar artwork to the album "Square Peg In A Round Hole", a red lit up translucent square over a white circle against a black background. The reverse is white with "Apparatjik" in large red letters at the top and track information in black below. The disc is plain with black text and "Apparatjik" in larger font at the top.
