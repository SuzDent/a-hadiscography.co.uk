Wash Her Off Your Hands (12.98)

This is a Norwegian one-track promo CD in a card title sleeve. The front sleeve is white with a small red rectangle in the centre, on which the text "1732 Høtten" is printed in yellow letters.

 

The reverse of the sleeve is also white with the text "furuholmen / bjerkestrand / wadling wash her off your hands" plus other information printed in black across the sleeve.

The disc is also white with black titles. "not for sale" is printed across the bottom of the disc, showing that it is to be used for promotional purposes only.
