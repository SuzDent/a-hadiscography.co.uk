2cU Shine (09.05.05)

The second single "2cU Shine" from Magne's album "Past Perfect Future Tense" was released in the UK on 9 May 2005. This single is not readily available in the stores, but can be ordered online through www,magne-f.co.uk. Both the regular release and promo are 1-track CDs. The CD insert is blue with some of Mangne's artwork down the left hand side. The title is printed in white in the top left hand corner.

The version available through Magne's website has "PROMO COPY" printed on the sleeve. The inside of the insert features a live shot of Magne on stage with a photograph of Magne projected over his face. Track and copyright information is printed in white at the bottom of the sleeve. The disc is plain silver with magne f and the title printed in respectively white and black letters. The "Private and Confidential Records" logo is printed in white on the left of the disc. The catalogue number is printed in black at the bottom of the disc.

The UK made promo comes in a transparent sleeve with a one sided card insert which is similar to the sleeve of the regular release. The disc is white with black text plus a red "Private and Confidential Records" logo. It doesn't have a catalogue number.
