Magne Furuholmen - There Goes Another Year (01.11.2019)

Magne released his third single "There Goes Another Year" from his album "White Xmas Lies". This is a digital single supporting the Norwegian Salvation Army (Frelsesarmeen). The artwork for the single features a broken metallic red bauble with a gold top lying on a white background. The title is in white cut-out hand-written font across the bauble. The Norwegian Salvation Army (Frelsesarmeen) logo is printed in the top right. It features just the one track "There Goes Another Year" (05:08) taken from the album.
