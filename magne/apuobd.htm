Magne Furuholmen - A Punchup On Boxing Day (26.12.2019)

Magne released his fifth single "A Punch-up On Boxing Day" from his album "White Xmas Lies". This is a digital single. The artwork for the single features what looks like a dark rectangular metal plate on a blue background with a handle at one end and the title in large pastel coloured (blue, green grey, orange) cut-out hand-written letters of differing sizes. It features just the one track "A Punch-up On Boxing Day" (04:58) taken from the album.
