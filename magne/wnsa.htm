We'll Never Speak Again (02.97?)

This one track promo only CD single was released in Norway. It is the second promo CD released from the soundtrack album "Hotel Oslo". This track features a duet between Freddie Wadling and Anneli M. Drecker (from "Bel Canto").

The cover of this promo features a red-one picture of a hand ringing a table bell. The title is written across the middle in black text and "Hotel Oslo" is printed in white on a purple strip in the bottom right hand corner. The disc is black with the title printed in silver letters on a blue strip across the top.
