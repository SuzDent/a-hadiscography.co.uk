Nothing Here To Hold You (2005)

This promo only CD single from Italy features the "Radio Version" (3:24) of "Nothing Here To Hold You". It comes with an insert in a PVC wallet. The front of the insert shows a close up brown-toned shot of Magne wearing a white striped shirt. Magne F is printed in yellow letters on the left hand side with the title in white below. The back of the sleeve is brown with Magne F and the title in respectively white and yellow with the track and contact information in white below. "Promotional copy, not for sale" is printed in white at the bottom. There is also a silver holographic sticker on the back. It was released by FMA Edizioni Musicali, Italy.
