Love Comes Shining Over The Mountains (09.08.99)

This compilation album features one song by Timbersound (Furuholmen / Bjerkestrand) which is a new mix of track 9 "Solve Et Coagula 09" from the album "Hermetic".

The album was released by the record company "Rune Grammofon" and consists of 78 minutes of "the finest contemporary electronics from both well known and unknown Norwegian artists". All tracks / mixes are unique to this album. The album cover is purple with a black and white abstract design by Kim Hiortøy.
